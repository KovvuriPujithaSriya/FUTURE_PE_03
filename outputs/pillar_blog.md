# Frequently Asked Questions

## How often should I visit a beauty salon?

Most people visit a salon every 4–6 weeks for hair care and monthly for skincare treatments.

## Are salon facials good for the skin?

Yes. Professional facials help improve skin texture, hydration, and overall appearance.

## What is a hair spa treatment?

A hair spa is a deep-conditioning treatment that nourishes hair and improves scalp health.

## How early should bridal makeup be booked?

It is recommended to book bridal makeup services at least 1–3 months before the wedding date.
